How to handle Dog2 / Blazing Footfalls: The start of Dog2 is easy and we've got this part down: - phase starts off with us using knockback prevention, just like Dog1. - next he'll jump N/S with an aoe or knockback (we run past A or C) - Diflare or Tetraflare tells us if party stacks or pair stacks - the first telegraph tells us if we group on the left or right of the boss for the first 'push' (his north-to-south dash that knocks our parties/pairs to an edge) Where it starts getting dicey is the Blazing Footfalls mechanic - this helped me get my brain wrapped around it. (refer to image below) When the first push happens, there's a bunch of weird stuff that goes on, but what you're really worried about is getting to the correct A or C before his second push (east-to-west dash). Here's how you do that: 1. At first push, pop Sprint and you'll run to one of four spots (two for if aoe is first, two for if knockback is first, one each if north is safe so wanna end at A, and one each if south is safe so wanna end at C) 2. Once you're at the correct spot, you're just waiting for the aoe/knockback before running to A/C. Hug the black line there for his second push. 3. After that push, you're in the right spot to either: 1) dodge an aoe and go east or west for torches, or 2) line up a knockback to an east/west corner for torches. Knowing that you're popping Sprint and running to one of these four spots, and then running to A or C - really simplified this, for me at least. (edited)
