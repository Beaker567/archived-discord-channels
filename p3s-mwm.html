===== P3S Fountain of Fire (30s of video below shows how this works) * Starting positions are relative to one of the two towers at the start. - Towers will start either N & S or E & W. - If you're in Core's group for sunbirds, key off the N (or E) tower. - If you're in Zil's group for sunbirds, key off the S (or W) tower. - Tank start: should be the only 2 inside boss target circle, can cheat towards their first towers. - Ranged start: clock 90° CW from 1st tower, position just outside of where your tower (3rd) will land, on the side closest to arena center. - Melee start: behind boss for uptime, in melee range but must be outside of target circle - Healer start: outside half of N&S (or E&W) towers. * Tank/Melee/Ranged: in pairs, order will be tanks > melee > ranged. On your turn (happens fast, move in when prior pair moves out): - be inside target circle to catch debuff (except ranged), avoid any dirty being dropped - once debuffed, drop dirty in front of your tower - wait for the prior tether to dash, then stretch your tether (run to other side of target circle and shift a little to your left/CCW) - after your bird dashes, can position as you like, just avoid dirty and tethers * Healer: soak 4 successive towers. Stay on outer part of the soak zone for the first 3 (to avoid dirty when birds are dropped), move into the inner part of the last tower soak (no bird drop on last tower).
