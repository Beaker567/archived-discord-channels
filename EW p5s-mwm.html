Bodhi Blackhand 09-Sep-22 04:12 PM
Silv found the original graphic. I flipped it so the boss would be pointing left and I could throw all our names on it. (Zil is tanking at that point of the fight, and he's in the 'left group'.) Our left and right groups each have a melee/ranged/tank/heal. But for this uptime strat, you want both melee on the current tank's side. The result is the OT can take the right melee's old spot and maintain melee range. Since our ranged are BRD and SMN, we prefer the BRD to do all the running to swap sides, not the caster. There's other shifts in the left group, but you can see all that on the graphic. --- Regarding crystal spawn points, I'll add to what Benji said: could be mirrored, and I suspect could be clocked 90 degrees - so lots of variance there. The way some (all?) of us handle it, is going into the mechanic, left group positions in lower left quadrant and right group in upper right quadrant. For those in the lower left, the closest topaz crystal will spawn at either the S or W cardinal, then we move behind it. After topaz explodes (wait for the floor to flash!), the rest of the spacing for me and my 'left group' buddies resolves itself (healer sprint to far corner, left melee on wall/poison, right melee close to middle, MT between the three of us). The right group spreads in a similar fashion, but with OT at mid, ranged on walls, and healer between the three of them. (edited)
