Everyone goes there. 2nd step: Do I have a tether? -If I do NOT have a tether > I'm staying on this platform. -If I do HAVE a tether > I'm looking to see which minotaur on the other two platforms I'm tethered to. For the PPL who HAVE a tether. You look to see which mino you are tethered to. You then go the opposite platform of that mino to stretch out your tether. There are multiple ways you can logically process how to think about this. Do whatever works best for you. (and vice versa) If I were tethered to the bottom mino on the right platform, I go to the top mino on the left platform. (and vice versa) (edited)
